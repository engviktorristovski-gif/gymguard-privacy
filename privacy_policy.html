Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

GymGuard is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

GymGuard does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not collect usage analytics or crash reports
  • We do not use advertising identifiers

2. Sensor Data

GymGuard uses your device's accelerometer and gyroscope sensors to detect phone movement and trigger the alarm. This sensor data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device in real time
  • Never stored or saved beyond the immediate detection calculation
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party

3. Alarm History

GymGuard Pro includes an alarm history feature that logs when the alarm was triggered. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never uploaded or shared
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

4. In-App Purchases

GymGuard Pro is available as a one-time in-app purchase through Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment or billing information.

5. Permissions

GymGuard requests the following Android permissions:

  • BODY_SENSORS / HIGH_SAMPLING_RATE_SENSORS — to access the accelerometer and gyroscope for motion detection
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — to keep the alarm active when the screen is locked
  • WAKE_LOCK — to prevent the device from sleeping while armed
  • VIBRATE — for WearOS watch vibration alerts
  • USE_BIOMETRIC / USE_FINGERPRINT — for biometric authentication to unlock the app
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS — to set maximum volume when the alarm triggers
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to restore alarm state after device restart
None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data.

6. Third-Party Services

GymGuard does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The following Google services are used solely for app functionality:

  • Google Play Billing — for processing the GymGuard Pro in-app purchase
  • Google Wearable Data Layer API — for communication between the app and a paired WearOS watch (no personal data is transmitted)

7. Children's Privacy

GymGuard is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Data Security

Since GymGuard does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no personal data at risk. All app preferences and alarm history are stored locally using Android's standard secure storage mechanisms.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of GymGuard after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
